About

Health2gain provides tools to measure and monitor personal health experiences. Do you want to assess and visualize your current health and wellbeing? Have a look at your Health Disc for free! 

The Health Disc (Psychosomatic Assessment Health Disc) has been developed by Dr. Christian Fazekas and his research team at Medical University of Graz/Division of Medical Psychology, Psychosomatics and Psychotheraphy in Austria.
